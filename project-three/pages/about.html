About Us

Dubuque Drumworks is a percussion store and dealer located in Dubuque, Iowa. The store opened in 2014 and carries a wide variety of percussion from a wide variety of brands. In the future, we look forward to expanding and becoming more widely known when providing our instruments to the Dubuque area. We are very passionate about percussion and have a commitment to share it with our community and the surrounding area.

Where Passion and Tradition Converge

At Dubuque Drumworks, we are more than just a percussion store; we are a testament to the enduring love of music that has been passed down through generations. Our family-owned and operated business has been an integral part of the Dubuque music community for decades. With a commitment to excellence and an unwavering passion for rhythm, we have become a trusted destination for percussion enthusiasts of all backgrounds.

A Legacy of Musical Dedication

Our story begins with a family deeply rooted in the music industry. Through the years, we've developed a profound appreciation for the artistry, innovation, and craftsmanship that define the world of percussion. This legacy is the cornerstone of Dubuque Drumworks, driving our dedication to providing you with the best products and unparalleled service.

A Journey from Humble Beginnings

In 2014, Dubuque Drumworks opened its doors to the rhythm-hungry community of Dubuque, Iowa. Initially, we carried just a handful of brands and products, but our dream was boundless. As our passion met the demand of our valued customers, we expanded our offerings, cultivating a diverse selection of brands and products to satisfy the needs of musicians and drummers at all levels.

The Dubuque Drumworks Difference

What sets us apart is our unwavering commitment to excellence. We handpick every item in our inventory, ensuring that it meets the highest standards of quality. We take pride in offering only the best instruments, drum sets, accessories, and equipment, guaranteeing your satisfaction and allowing your passion for percussion to thrive.

Community and Connection

Dubuque Drumworks is more than just a store; it's a hub for like-minded individuals who share a love for music and rhythm. We value the connections we make with our customers, fellow musicians, and the Dubuque community. Through workshops, events, and our collaborative spirit, we aim to foster a vibrant music scene right here in Dubuque.
